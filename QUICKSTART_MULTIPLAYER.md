## ğŸ® First Steps in Multiplayer Mode

### 1. Starting Your Empire
When you first load multiplayer.html:
- You start with **1 home planet** (Sol Prime)
- You have **5,000 credits**, **200 energy**, **1,000 metals**
- Your rank is **Explorer** (lowest tier)

### 2. Build Your First Scout Ship (Turns 1-10)
1. Click **Fleet** tab
2. Scroll to **Scout Ship** 
3. Click **Build** button
4. Wait 5 turns for construction
5. Ship appears in your fleet when ready

**Why Scout First?**
- Fastest ship (speed 10)
- Cheapest to build
- Best for exploration
- +30% bonus for finding rare resources

### 3. Explore Your First System (Turns 6-15)
1. Go to **Galaxy Map** tab
2. Click on any **undiscovered system** (â“ icon)
3. In the system details panel:
   - Select your Scout Ship from dropdown
   - Click **Send Ship**
4. Wait for ship to arrive (shown in alerts)
5. Earn **+100 Prestige** for discovery!

### 4. Build a Colony Ship (Turns 16-30)
1. Go to **Fleet** tab
2. Build **Colony Ship** (requires 500 population)
3. Wait 10 turns for construction
4. Send Colony Ship to a discovered system

### 5. Establish Your Second Colony (Turns 31+)
1. Navigate to **Galaxy Map**
2. Find system with your Colony Ship
3. Click **Colonize** on any planet
4. Earn **+500 Prestige**
5. **Unlock invasion capability!** (2 planets required)
